Cycle the Continental Divide

2018 - September 3rd

For the ultimate in adventure, join us for our annual “Cycle the Continental Divide” event. Registered participants will be shuttled to Targhee Pass to begin the ride. Our route follows the Continental Divide Trail (#115), gaining elevation quickly. Trail #115 ascends Lionhead Mountain in the Henry’s Lake Range. We’ll continue on the CDT, taking Trail #215 to the highest part of the trail, 10,000 in elevation, on Targhee Peak. The downhill portion has over 70 switchbacks and gorgeous views of the Centennial Range.
